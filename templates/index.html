AI Based Water Crisis Risk Prediction System

Water Crisis Risk Analysis

This system predicts the possibility of a water crisis using Machine Learning.

The prediction is based on:

  • Rainfall: Amount of annual rainfall in the region.
  • Population: Number of people depending on available water resources.
  • Groundwater: Indicator of underground water availability.

Lower rainfall, lower groundwater availability and higher population generally increase the risk of a water crisis.

How Prediction Works

The system uses a Random Forest Machine Learning model trained on historical rainfall, population and groundwater datasets.

During training, the model learns patterns associated with water availability and water crisis risk.

When the user enters new values, the trained model analyzes the inputs and predicts whether the region has a High Risk or Low Risk of a future water crisis.
